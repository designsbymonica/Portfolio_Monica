Hygge

Cultural and artistic magazine produced with Almudena Redondo, Eva Gallego, Aymé Julián and Carlos Huertas.

Tools: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom.

The theme of the magazine revolves around the concept of ‘Hygge’. It is a Danish philosophy of life that focuses on appreciating the small pleasures of life, the little things and, above all, finding happiness in them.

This concept is reflected in various items, in which we have represented those little things in life that we appreciate, enjoy and that make us happy.

Furthermore, the idea is for the magazine to be a serialised product, meaning that future editions can be produced to retain our readers.

To achieve all this, we have made extensive use of photography, as it plays a very important role in our magazine due to its visual impact.
