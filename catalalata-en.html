Escamas y Glifos

Finalist in the 7th edition of the ANFACO Design Award

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop.

Design of a collection of tinned fish and seafood cans by ANFACO-CYTMA, in collaboration with the Banco Sabadell Foundation.

The project consists of designing the packaging for three canned fish and seafood products, with experimentation with typography and illustration as the common thread. Using different colour palettes, each tin reflects a coherent and attractive visual experience.
