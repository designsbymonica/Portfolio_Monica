Lyra

Bachelor's Thesis. Multimedia and Graphic Design. UDIT 2025

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign.

This project focuses on the design and development of a mobile application for personal organisation and planning aimed at professionals and students in the creative field and related disciplines, whose unique selling point is effective customisation.

Lyra arose from the need for specific tools adapted to the particularities of the sector, characterised by dynamic projects, tight deadlines and non-linear creative processes. Based on prior user research, this tool allows creatives to choose the combination of functions and aesthetics that best suit their daily planning, so that users can focus their attention and energy on achieving their goals in a balanced way.

Lyra is designed to accompany creatives in their daily lives, but it not only organises, it also guides and inspires. Its approachable and carefully crafted visual identity is an extension of that philosophy. Every design decision responds to a system designed to connect with the creative community effectively.

Ultimately, Lyra reflects the possibility of designing with empathy and purpose. Instead of assuming that we must adapt to rigid systems, we must argue that it is the tool that must adapt to our rhythms, needs and ways of working. The journey has been as valuable as the end result, allowing me to consolidate tools and methodology and develop a more mature design vision that is conscious and connected to people.
