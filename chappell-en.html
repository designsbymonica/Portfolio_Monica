Chappell Roan Lyric Video

Motion graphics spot for Spotify with After Midnights by Chappell Roan.

Tools: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator.

This project consists of a motion graphics spot developed in After Effects as an academic exercise, conceived as a fictional commission for Spotify. The video is inspired by the visual universe of Chappell Roan, with a vibrant aesthetic in shades of blue and pink that reinforce the energy and freshness of the song After Midnights. The use of animated typography is combined with original vector illustrations, creating a dynamic visual narrative consistent with the artist's pop identity.

Throughout the advert, different animation techniques are used, with smooth graphic transitions connecting the scenes and maintaining a constant rhythm in tune with the music. The result is a lyric video that not only accompanies the song, but amplifies its atmosphere through colourful and contemporary visual language. The piece concludes with the claim ‘All your favourite tunes’, aligning with Spotify's communicative tone and reinforcing the idea of musical diversity and accessibility.
