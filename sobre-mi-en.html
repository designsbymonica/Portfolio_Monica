About me

Hello! My name is Monica and I am a graphic designer currently living in Madrid.

My passion for design was born from my interest in telling stories through images, which today allows me to work with graphic resources and visual narratives to convey ideas and emotions. I see design as a tool for connecting with people, which is why I design with empathy and purpose, seeking solutions that are not only aesthetic, but also meaningful and useful.

The tools I use most in my work are Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Adobe Lightroom.
