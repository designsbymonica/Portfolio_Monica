Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood: A guide through the most relevant artists

Conceptualisation and design of a collection of books exploring the relevant artists of the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign.

The design consists of a die-cut cover that reveals fragments of iconic paintings by each of the artists through geometric stripes. In turn, the design uses colour gradients inspired by this movement, which took inspiration from the Quattrocento.

In this way, the collection transcends its documentary function to become an evocative publication, capable of conveying the artistic depth of its protagonists and the authenticity that characterised the group.
