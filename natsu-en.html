Natsu

Ideation and design of a premium tea brand and its individual packaging.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro.

NATSU is a premium tea brand inspired by the Japanese philosophy of balance: between tradition and modernity, calm and energy, earth and air. The brand aims to connect a new generation with the ritual of tea through a contemporary, relaxed, and sensory approach.

The name “NATSU” (夏) means summer in Japanese, evoking freshness, clarity, and well-being, while maintaining a deep connection with the cultural heritage of tea.

The goal was to design a visual identity that communicates the classic elegance of traditional tea with a modern, light, and refreshing touch. It should appeal both to regular tea drinkers and to new consumers seeking an aesthetic, sensory, and balanced experience.

"La calma se sirve"
